## What SmartDecrypt PDF ZIP Does

If you haven't used it yet, here's what [SmartDecrypt PDF ZIP](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/smartdecrypt-pdf-zip/id6763979229) actually does.

### Unlock Protected Files Without Typing Passwords Every Time

SmartDecrypt stores named password profiles using the Apple Keychain — the same secure credential store your browser uses. You create a profile once, tie it to a filename pattern (for example, `statement-*.pdf` or `payslip-*.zip`), and the app matches the right password automatically when you drop a file. No copy-pasting from a notes app. No hunting through old emails.

### Supported File Formats

SmartDecrypt handles the three formats that consistently cause problems:

- **PDF** — password-protected PDFs from banks, payroll providers, government agencies, and document archives
- **ZIP** — encrypted ZIP archives used for comic downloads, document bundles, and file transfers
- **7z** — the open-source 7-Zip format, common for large archives and multi-part compressed files

### Batch Decrypt With One Tap

With SmartDecrypt Pro (a one-time universal purchase for iOS and macOS), you can load multiple files at once and let the app cycle through saved profiles automatically. Drop 20 payslips, apply your salary profile, and the app processes every file in sequence — entirely on-device, nothing sent to any server.

### Keychain-Safe Password Storage

Passwords are stored in the Apple Keychain on iOS and macOS. On Windows, they're stored in the system credential manager. SmartDecrypt never writes passwords to a database file or sends them to a server. If you uninstall the app, the passwords stay in your keychain and can be retrieved or cleared through the system's own credential manager.

### 100% On-Device Processing

This is the part most apps get wrong. Several PDF unlocker tools online require you to upload your document to their servers. SmartDecrypt does nothing of the sort — decryption runs locally using a Rust FFI core. Your files never leave your device.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does SmartDecrypt work with Chinese, Japanese, or Korean filenames?**
Yes. Pattern matching is fully Unicode-aware and handles CJK filenames correctly. The Simplified Chinese UI localisation is a separate addition — the underlying matching engine already supported CJK filenames before this release.

**How do I switch the app language?**
The app follows your device's system language automatically. On iOS: Settings → General → Language & Region. On macOS: System Settings → General → Language & Region. On Windows: Settings → Time & Language → Language & Region. Restart SmartDecrypt after changing the setting.

**Is SmartDecrypt free?**
SmartDecrypt is free for unlocking one file at a time using pattern matching. SmartDecrypt Pro is a one-time universal purchase (covers both iOS and macOS) that unlocks batch file loading and automatic password cycling. There is no subscription.

**Is SmartDecrypt available on Windows?**
Yes. SmartDecrypt is on the Microsoft Store for Windows. You can find it alongside the iOS and Mac versions.

**Is my data sent anywhere?**
No. All decryption runs locally on your device using a Rust FFI core. Files, passwords, and results never leave your device. There is no cloud component, no analytics, and no account required.
