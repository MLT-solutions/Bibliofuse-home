Read your way

  • Text-to-speech - hands-free narration for EPUB and TXT.
  • Auto-scroll - paged or continuous, tuned for image archives.
  • Peek zoom - double-tap to inspect dense panels and small text.
Mac streams to mobile

Library hub. Streams to your iPhone.

No third-party server · No subscription

On Mac, BiblioFuse becomes a desktop library hub: manage local folders, iCloud folders and SMB network shares in a split-view library/reader interface. Then stream books straight to BiblioFuse on iPhone and iPad over local Wi-Fi at home, or remotely over 5G when you're out.

  • Local Wi-Fi streaming - instant browse from any Mac source.
  • Remote 5G streaming - open your home library from anywhere with Tailscale.
  • Local, iCloud & SMB sources - one Mac library, every folder you already have.
  • Optional PIN - lock streaming access on shared networks.

Remote access requires iCloud signed in and Tailscale connected on both Mac and iOS. We also recommend using Tailscale for local Wi-Fi connections by selecting the device name with "(iCloud)".
