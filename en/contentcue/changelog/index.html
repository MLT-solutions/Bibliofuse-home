ContentCue

Release history, known issues, and what's coming next.

2026-07-031.0.2
iOS & macOSRelease

v1.0.2 — RSS Reader, Podcast Player & Website TTS

ContentCue is now an all-in-one browse, read, and listen app. Added RSS Reader with pinned channels, OPML import/export, and a continuous TTS briefing of unread headlines. Added Podcast Player with a built-in search directory, offline downloads, lock-screen controls, and a sleep timer. Added Website TTS with background audio playback that continues when your screen is locked. Added iCloud Sync for bookmarks, RSS feeds, and podcast subscriptions across iPhone and iPad. Added Siri Shortcuts ("Hey Siri, play ContentCue") with separate source and speed controls for RSS and Podcasts. Existing browser features — position restore, visual ad distinction, audio mute, reading mode, stay-in-browser, and full-page translation — are unchanged. Premium is now available as a yearly subscription with a 7-day free trial, or a one-time lifetime purchase.

2026-06-261.0.1
iOS & macOSRelease

v1.0.1 — 11-language localization

Added support for up to 11 app languages, with French, Dutch, Russian, Japanese, Korean, and Malay joining English, Spanish, Portuguese, Indonesian, and Simplified Chinese. Refined Premium, settings, legal/support links, and reading controls for the App Store release.

2026-05-271.0.0
iOS & macOSRelease

v1.0 — Premium, bookmarks & reading controls

One-time Pro unlock ($2.99) with unlimited bookmarks, custom ad border styles, and adjustable auto-scroll speed. Reading Mode strips page clutter for a cleaner view. Stay-in-browser blocks app-redirect prompts that pull you out of a site mid-read. Full-page translation powered by iOS 18 (requires iOS 18 or later). Redirect controls. Localized app text across all supported languages.

2026-05-161.0.0
iOS & macOSRelease

v1.0 — Initial launch

App Store launch. An iOS browser that saves your scroll position so you can pick up exactly where you left off when an ad interrupts. Tap Resume to return to your saved position on any site. Colored borders highlight in-body ads (visual distinction — ads still display and load fully). Audio mute toggle for quiet reading. Works with Webtoons, Tapas, manga sites, and any web content.
