FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ContentCue an ad blocker?

No. ContentCue does not block, hide, or prevent ads from loading. All advertisements display normally. ContentCue improves navigation by saving your scroll position when ads interrupt your reading, and it draws colored borders around in-page ads so you can see them clearly.

Which sites does ContentCue work with?

ContentCue is a full web browser, so it works with any website — Webtoons, Tapas, LINE Webtoon, independent creator pages, manga aggregators, WordPress blogs, and thousands more. No per-site setup is needed.

How does position restore work?

ContentCue monitors your scroll position while you read. When you navigate away (tapping an ad link, a redirect, or the back button), it saves your exact position. A Resume button appears in the browser UI. Tap it to return to exactly where you were.

Does ContentCue have an RSS reader?

Yes. Subscribe to any RSS feed, pin your favourite channels, and import or export your whole subscription list via OPML. Turn on TTS to hear your unread headlines read aloud as one continuous audio briefing, like a personal radio update.

Can I listen to podcasts in ContentCue?

Yes. Search the built-in podcast directory, pin the shows you follow, and stream or download episodes to listen offline. Playback works from the lock screen and includes a built-in sleep timer.

Does ContentCue read web pages aloud?

Yes. Tap Play on any article and ContentCue reads it aloud with text-to-speech. Background audio keeps playing when your screen is locked, and the app quickly resumes your last article and position.

Does ContentCue sync across my iPhone and iPad?

Yes, with Premium. Bookmarks, RSS feeds, and podcast subscriptions sync automatically via iCloud across all your Apple devices.

Does ContentCue collect my browsing data?

No. ContentCue processes all browsing entirely on your device using Apple WebKit. No browsing history, visited URLs, or personal data is collected or transmitted anywhere.

What does ContentCue Premium include?

Premium unlocks the full audio hub: RSS Reader with TTS and OPML import/export, Podcast Player with offline downloads, iCloud Sync across your devices, Siri Shortcuts, unlimited bookmarks, custom ad outline colours, and adjustable auto-scroll speed. It's available as a yearly subscription with a 7-day free trial, or a one-time lifetime purchase — price is shown in the app for your region.
