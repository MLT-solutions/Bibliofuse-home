FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does SmartDecrypt support?

PDF, ZIP, and 7z. Unencrypted ZIP and 7z archives are extracted normally without asking for a password. SmartDecrypt does not currently support RAR, EPUB, or other formats.

Does SmartDecrypt bypass unknown passwords?

No. SmartDecrypt is designed for files you already have permission to access. It uses passwords you have saved in a profile — it does not crack or brute-force unknown passwords.

Are my passwords safe?

Yes. Passwords are stored exclusively in the system Keychain on your device — the same secure storage used by Safari and your other apps. SmartDecrypt never stores passwords in its own database, and nothing is uploaded to any server.

What is a filename pattern?

A filename pattern is a partial name or keyword that identifies recurring files. For example, if your bank sends monthly statements named 'BankStatement_2024-01.pdf', create a profile with the pattern 'BankStatement' and the password. SmartDecrypt matches future files to that profile automatically.

Is the Pro purchase shared between iPhone and Mac?

Yes. SmartDecrypt Pro is a one-time universal purchase. Buy once on either iOS or macOS and unlock Pro on both platforms using the same Apple ID.
